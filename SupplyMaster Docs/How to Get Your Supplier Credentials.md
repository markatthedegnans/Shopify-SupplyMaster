SupplyMaster needs connection credentials to pull product data from your supplier. This guide tells you exactly what credentials each supplier requires and where to get them.

---

## S&S Activewear

| **Field** | **What to Enter** |
| --- | --- |
| Username | Your S&S Activewear Account Number |
| API Key | Your S&S Activewear API Key |
| Country | United States, Canada, or Puerto Rico |

[![The S&amp;S Activewear connection credentials form in SupplyMaster](https://comstack.intercom-attachments-1.com/i/o/eznrauif/2449551609/942c04b0f9391024d1451db314f2/ssa-credential-form.png?expires=1781227800&signature=23e669c3fc174409d733a2a6d88d14acc776074d1c0e3861d7709310cfe15928&req=diQjH8x7nIdfUPMW1HO4zRZNsRRURfB4Rg6b%2FeEqwTBLt57%2BnDElQSBCLXwm%0Aj9NbPzr%2BJiJ3DFGo7Dw%3D%0A)](https://comstack.intercom-attachments-1.com/i/o/eznrauif/2449551609/942c04b0f9391024d1451db314f2/ssa-credential-form.png?expires=1781227800&signature=23e669c3fc174409d733a2a6d88d14acc776074d1c0e3861d7709310cfe15928&req=diQjH8x7nIdfUPMW1HO4zRZNsRRURfB4Rg6b%2FeEqwTBLt57%2BnDElQSBCLXwm%0Aj9NbPzr%2BJiJ3DFGo7Dw%3D%0A)

1. Log in to your S&S Activewear account at **[https://www.ssactivewear.com/myaccount/](https://www.ssactivewear.com/myaccount/)**.
2. Your **Account Number** is displayed in your account profile — this is the Username in SupplyMaster.
3. On the same page, click **"Show API Key"** to reveal your API Key. Copy it and paste it into SupplyMaster.

If you don't see an API Key in your account, email **[api@ssactivewear.com](mailto:api@ssactivewear.com)** with your account number and request API access.

## SanMar (US)

| **Field** | **What to Enter** |
| --- | --- |
| FTP Username | Your SanMar FTP username |
| Password | Your SanMar FTP password |
| Country | United States |

SanMar FTP access is not self-service — you need to request it from SanMar directly:

1. Contact the SanMar Integration team at **[sanmarintegrations@sanmar.com](mailto:sanmarintegrations@sanmar.com)** or call **800-426-6399 x6458**.
2. Let them know you need FTP data feed access for a third-party integration.
3. SanMar will send you an Integration Agreement to sign and return.
4. Once approved, they'll provide your FTP username and password.

**Important:** Your FTP password may be different from your sanmar.com web login password. If you do not have your FTP username and password, contact SanMar at **[sanmarintegrations@sanmar.com](mailto:sanmarintegrations@sanmar.com)** or call **800-426-6399 x6458**.

## SanMar Canada

| **Field** | **What to Enter** |
| --- | --- |
| User Email | Your SanMar Canada API email |
| Account ID | Your SanMar Canada account ID |
| User Password | Your SanMar Canada API password |

1. Contact **SanMar Canada** directly and request that the API be enabled for your account.
2. Once enabled, use your SanMar Canada login email, account ID, and password as the credentials in SupplyMaster.

If the API has not been enabled for your account, it will not work until SanMar Canada activates it on their end.

## Cutter & Buck

Cutter & Buck does not require product-sync credentials — the catalog is sourced from a built-in data feed. Simply select **Cutter & Buck** as the supplier type, enter a name, and save.

If you plan to dropship and place orders automatically, separate **PromoStandards** credentials are entered later under **Edit Supplier** → **Order Settings** (not on the connection screen). See our **Syncing Orders with PromoStandards** article for setup details.

1. Email **[CBEDI@cutterbuck.com](mailto:CBEDI@cutterbuck.com)** and request PromoStandards API access for purchase order placement.
2. Once Cutter & Buck issues your PromoStandards Username and Password, enter them in **Edit Supplier** → **Order Settings**.
3. For PO or account questions, the same address (**[CBEDI@cutterbuck.com](mailto:CBEDI@cutterbuck.com)**) is the right contact.

## Suppliers That Connect Without Credentials

The following suppliers connect automatically and do not require any credentials for product sync. Simply select the supplier type when adding a new supplier, enter a name, and save.

| **Supplier** | **Notes** |
| --- | --- |
| Augusta Sportswear (Momentec) | No credentials needed — connects via the Momentec Augusta public data feed |
| Champro | No credentials needed |
| Decky | No credentials needed |
| AS Colour US | No credentials needed |
| Cutter & Buck | No product-sync credentials needed; PromoStandards credentials for orders are entered separately under Order Settings (see above) |

For these suppliers, the **Test Credentials** step is skipped automatically. You can proceed directly to configuring inventory settings and product filters after saving.

## Custom Supplier

If your supplier is not on the list above, you can submit a **Custom Supplier** request. Provide all credential fields available to you (Username, Password, Additional Info) — these will be shared with our support team. After you save the custom supplier, our team will assess the integration and email you an update within 3 business days.

## Troubleshooting

- Verify that the account credentials entered are accurate — credentials are case-sensitive.
- Make sure you're using the right credential type (e.g., SanMar requires *FTP* credentials, which may be different from your sanmar.com website login).
- For S&S Activewear, confirm you're entering your *Account Number* as the Username, not your email address.
- For SanMar Canada, confirm the API has been enabled for your account by the SanMar team.
- If needed, contact the supplier directly to verify your account credentials.
- For further support, contact support on chat in the bottom-right corner of the app.
